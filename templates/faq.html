Frequently Asked Questions

We've got answers to all your burning questions. Well, most of them, anyway.

What is this website all about?

We're a website that helps you find gigs. Yeah, it's a real thing.

How do I use this website?

Uh, just click on stuff and see what happens. Seriously, it's not that hard.

Is this website free?

Yes, it's free. But don't worry, we won't make you watch ads or anything (unless you want to, in which case, we'll make you watch ads).

What kind of gigs can I find on this website?

All kinds of gigs. Okay, fine. We have a few categories, but you can search for whatever you want.

How do I apply for a gig?

Just click on the gig and follow the instructions. It's not rocket science.

Can I post my own gig?

Yes, you can post your own gig. Just make sure it's not a scam or anything.

What if I have a question that's not answered here?

Well, that's a great question. You can contact us and we'll try to answer it. Or we'll just make something up.
